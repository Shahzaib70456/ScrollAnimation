The Woodcutter's Story

1

Once there lived a poor woodcutter. His axe fell down into the river and he could not take the axe out.

2

Mercury, the god of Water, appeared and asked the reason for his weeping. The woodcutter told the whole story. Mercury dived into the water and brought a golden axe, but the woodcutter refused to take it.

3

Mercury again dived and brought a silver axe. The woodcutter again refused.

4

Then she brought an iron axe. The woodcutter took it gladly. Mercury was so pleased that she rewarded the woodcutter with the other two axes.
